## What's Changed

### Added
- Search results now support multi-select batch favoriting, and you can swipe a single result to favorite it quickly

### Changed
- Tasks page: background tasks such as WebDAV sync now show unified titles and status, with options to manage and clear task history
- Reading settings: a new collapsible "Gesture settings" section groups Double-tap zoom, Long-press zoom, Long-press zoom position, and Volume-key page turning

### Fixed
- Fixed imported local comics not showing their cover on the details and history pages
- Fixed garbled / incorrect Chinese text on some Android devices (e.g. ColorOS 16)
- Smoother loading when opening the History / Follow-Updates pages
- Clearing history now clears records for all task types
- Fixed the paused-task icon and stutter on the tasks page

**Full Changelog**: https://github.com/Kyosee/venera/compare/v2.1.0...v2.1.1
