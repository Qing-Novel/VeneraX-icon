## What's Changed

### Added
- Image favorites: new grid layout with a toggle button next to search. Choice persists across restarts.

### Changed
- Local comics now share the unified comic detail entry and load local data first.
- Web client: continue-reading button shows whenever a last-read chapter exists.

### Fixed
- Image favorites: fixed thumbnails not matching their comic after reordering.
- Local comic download: fixed two defects in the local comic download path.
- Web client: reading history now refreshes correctly when returning from the reader.
- Local comics: fixed a potential null-check crash when opening a downloading comic.
